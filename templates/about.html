FAQ

Showing all FAQs

How do I use speech input? (toggle)

Click the microphone, speak clearly. On iOS use Chrome/Edge. Interim words appear, final transcript converts to signs.

Why isn‚Äôt Safari speech working? (toggle)

Safari lacks reliable Web Speech API support. Install Chrome or Edge on iOS for microphone features.

Will offline mode be available? (toggle)

Yes. PWA support + cached sign videos are planned so classrooms with limited connectivity can still function.

Which languages are fully supported now? (toggle)

Currently 12+: Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, Odia, Assamese & English. More will be added based on sign asset availability.

Are my inputs stored or tracked? (toggle)

No personal speech/text is saved long‚Äëterm. Future saved sessions will be an explicit opt‚Äëin feature with transparency.

Why do some words not have a sign video? (toggle)

ISL often uses conceptual keywords instead of every word. The system extracts key signable terms. Missing assets are queued for creation.

Does playback work on all mobile browsers? (toggle)

Yes for video playback. Microphone works best on Chrome (Android/iOS) and Edge. Safari speech support is limited.

How can I contribute sign assets? (toggle)

Soon a contribution guide will allow creators to submit vetted ISL videos. Until then, use the contact form to express interest.

Will there be an avatar instead of videos? (toggle)

Exploration phase includes procedural signing avatars. This depends on quality, naturalness, and community validation.
